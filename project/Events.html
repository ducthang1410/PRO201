SHOW US YOUR WINNING FORMULA TO ENTER OUR LATEST GIVEAWAY

Title description, Dec 8, 2018

Event Details

Showcase your gaming reality or fantasy to celebrate the excellence of ROG motherboards, and win a Maximus X Formula or ROG gaming gear- now.

Designated modelsROG Maximus Formula ROG Maximus II Formula ROG Maximus III Formula ROG Maximus V Formula ROG Maximus VI Formula ROG Maximus VII Formula ROG Maximus VIII Formula ROG Maximus IX Formula ROG Maximus X Formula

Description

Do you own and adore the ROG Maximus Formula motherboard? Or do you lust after one? Want to snag the latest model? Showcase your gaming reality or fantasy for your chance to win Maximus X Formula and more!

The Maximus X Formula is the most recent chapter in a storied series of gaming motherboards that can be traced back to the original Maximus Formula from 2008. ROG has been devoted to engineering the best gaming hardware for more than a decade, and you can see the evidence throughout the Formula’s history. To celebrate this extraordinary milestone and exceptional board, we created the Your Winning Formula contest to give away Maximus X Formula motherboards, Strix GTX 1070 and 1070 Ti graphics cards, and other gaming hardware worth over $7,000 USD.
